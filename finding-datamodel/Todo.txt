Suggestions of what still to do:
 * review OncoAct and ACTIN implementations
 * review naming - at this stage this is still easy to change, at a later stage this will be undesirable.
 * decide if more logic can be moved to the findings datamodel
    - Some examples:
        - ACTIN applies a gene based filter on all findings
        - ACTIN splits TumorMutationStatus into TumorMutationalBurden and TumorMutationalLoad
        - ACTIN has some "variant dedup" logic
        - ACTIN converts proteinImpact to use singleLetterAminoAcids
        - ACTIN rounds copy numbers to 3 digits (different from OncoAct)

* Move handling of germline for NL into findings model?
